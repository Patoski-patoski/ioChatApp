  • How to use ioChatApp

    1. Sign up and login
    2. Add a friend registered on ioChatApp
    3. A unique code will be generated with your friend's username appended to it
    4. Send the code and your username to the friend as a handshake request
    5. Your friend accepts the handshake by adding your username
    6. Your friend adds your handshake
    7. Click "Chat" to start a conversation
    8. You can now send messages back and forth
